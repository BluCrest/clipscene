🎞️ The honest bit

AI can't actually watch videos yet. Clipscene works by analyzing the metadata, titles, descriptions, and web context attached to your URL. It works brilliantly for popular clips and viral moments, but may struggle with extremely obscure or unlabeled content. We're building toward true video analysis as the tech improves.
